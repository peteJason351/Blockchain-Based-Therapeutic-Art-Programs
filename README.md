# Therapeutic Art Platform

A comprehensive blockchain-based platform for coordinating art therapy sessions with material distribution, progress documentation, and therapeutic outcome tracking. The platform includes trauma-informed care protocols, artist mentor matching, exhibition opportunities, and healing journey visualization.

## 🌟 Key Features

### Core Functionality
- **Participant Registration**: Secure registration with trauma-informed consent and emergency contacts
- **Mentor Certification**: Art therapist registration with specialization and trauma-informed training
- **Session Management**: Create and coordinate art therapy sessions with material requirements
- **Progress Tracking**: Comprehensive documentation of therapeutic progress and outcomes
- **Material Distribution**: Track art supply distribution and participant feedback
- **Exhibition Platform**: Showcase participant artwork with healing stories

### Trauma-Informed Care
- **Safety Protocols**: Comprehensive safety incident reporting and crisis intervention
- **Therapeutic Assessments**: Detailed trauma assessments with trigger identification
- **Healing Journey Documentation**: Track artistic evolution and emotional growth
- **Outcome Measurement**: Quantitative tracking of therapeutic progress
- **Goal Setting**: Collaborative therapeutic goal setting and progress monitoring

## 🏗️ Architecture

### Smart Contracts

#### 1. Therapeutic Art Platform (`therapeutic-art-platform.clar`)
The main contract handling:
- Participant and mentor registration
- Session creation and enrollment
- Progress tracking and material distribution
- Exhibition management and artwork submissions
- Healing journey progress calculations

#### 2. Trauma-Informed Care (`trauma-informed-care.clar`)
Specialized contract for:
- Trauma assessments and safety planning
- Therapeutic outcome measurements
- Crisis intervention documentation
- Healing journey tracking
- Program effectiveness analytics

## 📋 Core Data Structures

### Participants
- Registration details with trauma consent
- Session attendance and progress tracking
- Emergency contacts and therapeutic goals
- Current skill level and total sessions completed

### Art Mentors
- Certification and specialization information
- Trauma-informed training credentials
- Session capacity and current participants
- Performance ratings and total sessions led

### Therapy Sessions
- Session details and material requirements
- Participant enrollment and attendance
- Progress documentation and mentor observations
- Therapeutic focus and session outcomes

### Exhibitions
- Curated showcases of participant artwork
- Submission approval and healing stories
- Therapeutic themes and participant engagement
- Community building through shared experiences
